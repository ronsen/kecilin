## Privacy Policy

Thank you for using **Kecilin** ("the App").

### Information Collection

Kecilin does **not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or user data**.

### Image Processing

All image resizing operations are performed locally on your device. Images selected for processing never leave your device and are not uploaded to any server.

### Third-Party Services

The App does not use analytics services, advertising networks, crash reporting tools, or any other third-party services that collect user data.

### Data Sharing

Since no user data is collected, no information is shared with any third parties.

### Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any information from users, including children under the age of 13.

### Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date above.
